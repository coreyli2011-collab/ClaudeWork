🎀 Bebefinn's Big Adventure! 🌟

Tap-and-explore game  ·  Ages 2–4  ·  🎵 Doo Doo Doo!

🌈 Tap Here to Play! 🌈

📱 iPhone & iPad Setup (do this once)

  1. Tap "Tap Here to Play!" above — it opens in Safari
  2. Turn the volume up on the device — the game talks to your child!
    (Make sure the phone is not on silent / ring switch is up)
  3. That's it — no login, no app download, just play! 🎉
💡 Optional — Add to Home Screen so it opens like an app:

In Safari, tap the Share button (the box with an arrow ↑ at the bottom of the screen) → scroll down → tap "Add to Home Screen" → tap "Add".
A Bebefinn icon will appear on the Home Screen — just like a real app!

💻 Android & PC (do this once)

  1. Click "Tap Here to Play!" above — it opens in your browser
  2. Turn the volume up before starting
  3. Play! 🎮
💡 On Android in Chrome, you can also tap the three-dot menu → "Add to Home screen" to get a shortcut icon on your phone's home screen.

🎮 How to Play

  1. Tap "Let's Go!" on the title screen
  2. The game asks "Who's playing today?" — tap the player's bubble
    Avery is always there by default; see below to add other names
  3. Pick one of the 6 adventure worlds — tap a picture to choose:
    • 🌸 Garden — flowers, butterflies, and bees
    • 🏖️ Beach — shells, crabs, and Baby Shark!
    • 🌳 Forest — bunnies, owls, and foxes
    • 🌈 Rainbow — collect all the colors
    • 🐄 Farm — cows, pigs, and baby chicks
    • 🚀 Space — stars, planets, and aliens
  4. The game says what to find out loud — your child just taps the matching pictures!
  5. Collect all 3 items → stars light up → confetti celebration! 🎊

Everything is tap and listen — no reading needed! 👶

👧 Adding a Guest Player

  1. From the "Who's playing?" screen, tap the + Add Friend circle
  2. A text box appears labelled "Grown-ups: type the name here" — type the name and tap Done ✓
  3. The new player appears as a coloured bubble and is remembered next time
  4. To remove a guest, tap the small ✕ badge on their bubble
💡 Avery is the default player and is always available — she cannot be removed. Guest names are saved on the device, so they come back automatically the next time you open the game.

🔊 Sound & Voice

The game uses your device's built-in voice to narrate everything — world names, what to find, item names when tapped, and a personalised celebration at the end.

A 🔊 mute button in the top-right corner lets you silence the narration at any time without stopping the game.

💡 For the clearest voice on iPhone/iPad, make sure Silent Mode is off (the switch on the side should not show an orange stripe) and volume is turned up before opening the game.
