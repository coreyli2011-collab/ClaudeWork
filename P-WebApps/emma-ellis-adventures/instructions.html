📱 iPhone & iPad Setup (do this once)

  1. Tap "Tap Here to Play!" above — it opens in Safari
  2. Turn the volume up — the game talks to Emma and Ellis!
  3. Make sure the silent switch is off (no orange stripe on the side)
  4. That's it — no login, no download, just play! 🎉
💡 Add to Home Screen for a shortcut icon:

In Safari tap the Share button (box with arrow ↑) → scroll down → "Add to Home Screen" → "Add".
An Emma & Ellis icon (❄️🦄) appears on the home screen!

💻 Android & PC (do this once)

  1. Click "Tap Here to Play!" above — opens in your browser
  2. Turn volume up before starting
  3. Play! 🎮
💡 On Android in Chrome: tap the three-dot menu → "Add to Home screen" to get an icon shortcut.

🎮 How to Play

  1. Tap "Let's Play!" on the title screen
  2. Tap your name (Emma, Ellis, together, or Guest)
  3. Choose a theme — Frozen World or Unicorn Kingdom
  4. Pick one of 6 adventure worlds — tap a picture!
    • Frozen: ❄️ Elsa's Palace · 🏰 Arendelle · 🏔️ North Mountain · ⛄ Olaf's Snowfield · 🌿 Enchanted Forest · 🌊 Frozen River
    • Unicorn: 🏰 Corona Castle · 🦄 Unicorn Meadow · 🏮 Lantern Festival · 🌲 Magic Forest · 🌈 Rainbow Valley · 🌟 Starlight Kingdom
  5. The game says out loud what to find — tap the matching pictures!
  6. Collect all 3 → stars light up → confetti celebration with their name! 🎊

Everything is tap and listen — no reading needed! 👧

⭐ Adding a Guest

  1. On the player screen, tap ⭐ Guest — Tap Here
  2. A text box appears: "Grown-ups: type the guest name here" — type the name and tap Done ✓
  3. The game greets the guest by name throughout!
💡 Guest names are not saved between sessions — a parent types the name fresh each time. This keeps it simple and private.

🔊 Voice & Sound

The game speaks everything out loud using the device's built-in voice — greetings, world descriptions, item names when tapped, and a personalised celebration.

A 🔊 mute button in the top-right corner silences the voice at any time without stopping the game.

💡 Tips for Parents

🎭 Two themes, 6 worlds each — 12 worlds total, keeping it fresh for a while

🔀 Each play randomly picks 3 items from a pool of 9, so the same world feels different every time

🤝 Emma & Ellis Together mode is great for sharing the device — same gameplay, both names called out

📵 Works offline once loaded in the browser

🌐 No accounts, no ads, no purchases — completely free
