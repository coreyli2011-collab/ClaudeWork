🦈✨ Princess & Baby Shark ✨🦈

Ocean Adventure Game  ·  Ages 4–6

🌊 Tap Here to Play! 🌊

📱 iPhone & iPad Setup (do this once)

  1. Tap "Tap Here to Play!" above — it opens in Safari
  2. When the game asks "Allow microphone?" — tap Allow
    (This only happens the very first time, ever)
  3. That's it — the game is ready! 🎉
💡 Optional — Add to Home Screen so it opens like an app:

Tap the Share button in Safari (the box with an arrow ↑ at the bottom of the screen) → scroll down → tap "Add to Home Screen" → tap "Add".
The game icon will appear on the Home Screen!

💻 PC Setup (do this once)

  1. Click "Tap Here to Play!" above — it opens in your browser
  2. When the browser asks about the microphone — click Allow
    (Only asked once)
  3. Play! 🎮

🎮 How to Play

  1. Baby Shark asks the girl her name — she says it out loud
  2. She becomes Princess [her name]!
  3. Choose her outfit — crown, dress colour, accessory
  4. Pick any of the 4 mini-games on the Ocean Map:
    • 🫧 Bubble Cove — pop bubbles to collect treasures
    • 🃏 Memory Reef — find matching pairs of cards
    • 🗺️ Treasure Cave — tap glowing spots to find hidden treasures
    • 🎵 Music Shell — tap sea creatures to make music

Everything is tap and voice — no reading or typing needed! 👧

💡 Tips

🔊 Turn volume up before starting
🤫 Speak clearly in a quiet room for best voice recognition
🔄 If Baby Shark doesn't catch the name after 3 tries, she gives the girl a fun princess name automatically
