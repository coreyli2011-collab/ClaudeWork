📲 How to add the game icon to your iPhone
  1. Tap the game link above to open it in Safari — or copy and paste it into the Safari address bar if it doesn't open automatically
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen — it looks like a box with an arrow pointing up ⬆️
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Turn on "Open as Web App" if it appears
  5. Tap Add — a crown icon 👑 will appear on your home screen
⚠️ Must use Safari — not Chrome.
If your iPhone opens the link in Chrome, copy the link, open the Safari app, and paste it into the address bar instead.
