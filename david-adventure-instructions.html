David's Great Adventure

A Bible story game for Emma & Ellis  💕

To save as PDF: click the button, then choose "Save as PDF" as the printer destination.

💻 Playing on a PC
  1. Find the file called david-adventure.html
  2. Double-click it — it opens in your web browser automatically
  3. Make sure your volume is turned up
  4. Tap who is playing and the game begins!
📱 Playing on an iPhone or iPad

Do these steps once to get the file onto the device:

  1. Email the file david-adventure.html to yourself as an attachment
  2. Open the email on the iPhone or iPad
  3. Tap and hold the attachment → tap Share → tap Save to Files
  4. Save it somewhere easy to find — for example, On My iPhone

To play after saving:

1. Open the Files app  →  find david-adventure.html  →  tap it

2. It opens in Safari — make sure volume is up, then tap who is playing!

📲 Adding a Home Screen Icon (Do Once)

This creates a game icon on the iPhone home screen — just like a real app. Children can tap it directly without opening Files.

  1. Follow the iPhone setup steps above to open the game in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen  (the box with an arrow pointing up  ⬆)
  3. Scroll down in the Share menu and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. The name will show as David's Adventure — tap Add in the top right
  5. A crown icon 👑 now appears on the home screen — tap it anytime to play!

✅  This only needs to be done once — the icon stays on the home screen permanently

✅  Tapping the icon opens the game directly in Safari — no Files app needed

✅  Works on both iPhone and iPad

🎮 How the Game Works
  • The game talks out loud — it tells children exactly what to do
  • Children simply tap what they see on the screen
  • There are 5 scenes following the story of David from the Bible
  • At the end, tap the glowing star ⭐ to hear personalised praise
  • Tap Play Again to restart from the beginning
📖 The 5 Scenes
🐑 Scene 1 · The Shepherd Boy
Tap all the sheep to collect them
🦁 Scene 2 · The Lion
Tap David to scare the lion away
👑 Scene 3 · God's Choice
Tap the golden crown
🪨 Scene 4 · Goliath the Giant
Tap David to throw the stone
🤴 Scene 5 · David Becomes King
Tap King David 5 times to celebrate!
💡 Tips

🔊  Works best with headphones or speaker volume turned up — the voice instructions are an important part of the game

📱  Best played in portrait mode (phone held upright)

🌐  No internet needed on PC  ·  Internet needed on iPhone / iPad

👂  The first tap unlocks the voice on iPhone — if no sound on the very first screen, tap any button and sound will start
